What it does

One platform for live teaching, pupil practice, and progress tracking.

Wordloom supports the everyday rhythm of classroom spelling while making follow-up more focused and evidence-led.

01

Teacher-built live tests

Quickly create and present spelling tests for whole-class teaching, not just individual pupil practice.

02

Pupil practice and follow-up

Pupils log in separately for assigned practice that can respond to baseline evidence and recent attempts.

03

Analytics and exports

Track progress over time, inspect patterns across pupils and words, and export useful data when schools need it.

Why it is different

Structured enough for schools. Flexible enough for teachers.

Baseline and placement first. Wordloom is designed to understand starting points before adding more practice.

Teacher-approved targeting. Word selection can stay thoughtful and classroom-aware, with follow-up linked to real evidence.

Insight beyond scores. Analytics can show developing areas, secure areas, review signals, and progress over time.

Built for normal lessons. Live classroom testing and presentation sit alongside independent pupil practice.
